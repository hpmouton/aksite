Our Core Values

FOCUS
AK Security is service-oriented, committed and keeps to its agreements. The Client is our central focus at all times. Surrounding the Client are the Employees and Quality. We strive to achieve a win-win situation. We treat our employees with respect and offer good terms of employment and provide training to promote their personal development.
INTEGRITY
AK Security is: transparent, sincere and careful. Our clients trust us and assume that we will treat their property, employees and clients with care. We do not want to damage this trust.
RELIABILITY
Above all, AK Security is reliable, professional, flexible and proactive. We are also an independent company with a strong fundamental base.
SAFETY
We are committed to the safety of all our employees and clients. We believe everything can be completed safely and strive for zero incidents.
TEAMWORK
We are one company, one team. We are committed to a teamwork environment where every person is a valued member. We believe in the inherent worth for all people.
